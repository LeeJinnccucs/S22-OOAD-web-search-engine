Project description Developing a user-friendly search engine from scratch. The search engine will focus on a specific domain of information to provide results confined to the knowledge space of that domain. The engine will serve through four primary functional units: Crawling/Spider - That will systematically browse the WWW to collect data for indexing. Indexing - refers to collecting, parsing, and storing data for fast retrieval. Searching - matching the user query to provide accurate results. Fronted end - presenting the retrieved data in a convenient and accessible manner.
